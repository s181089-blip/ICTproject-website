Screen Time and Mental Health

"Screen time" means how long we spent on our devices, it is usually a sedentary activity, meaning the person is sitting and not physically active.


"Mental health" refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

How are they connected

Spending too much time on screens can affect sleep, reduce physical activity, and limit real-life interaction. These changes can negatively impact a person’s mental health, leading to stress, anxiety, or low mood.

Why is it important?

Screen use is increasing every year, and many teenagers spend several hours a day on their devices and that is the reason why experts are concerned about its impact on mental health. High screen time is linked to anxiety and depression because it can reduce sleep quality and cause tiredness.
