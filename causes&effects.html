Causes (Why screen time is increasing):

  • Easy access to the internet
  • Social media apps designed to keep users engaged for long periods
  • Online school and remote work increasing daily screen use
  • Habit or addiction to scrolling, gaming, or watching videos
  • Fear of missing out (FOMO) on social media
  • Entertainment is easily available anytime (24/7 access)
  • Children are introduced to devices at a very young age
  • Parents sometimes use devices to keep children busy
  • Technology is now part of everyday life (education, communication, fun)

Effects (What happens because of it):

Mental Effects:

  • Anxiety and depression
  • Feeling stressed or overwhelmed
  • Addiction to phones, games, or social media
  • Feeling lonely even when online

Physical Effects:

  • Less physical activity and movement.
  • Eye strain and headaches
  • Poor posture (back and neck pain)
  • Sleep problems due to screen use at night.

Daily Habits:

  • Shorter attention span.
  • Difficulty focusing on school or tasks.
  • Poor sleep or staying up late.
  • Less interest in hobbies or outdoor activities.

Long-term Effects:

  • Ongoing mental health problems.
  • Social isolation.
  • Poor lifestyle habits.
  • Reduced thinking and problem-solving skills.
